* 1 large head of romain or mixed greens
* ⅓ cup blue cheese, crumbled
* ⅓ cup dry roasted pistachios
* ⅓ cup dried cherries
* 1 8oz. can mandarin oranges, drained
* 2 ½ tbsp balsamic vinegar
* ¼ cup virgin olive oil
* 1 ½ tbsp honey
* Salt and pepper to taste
